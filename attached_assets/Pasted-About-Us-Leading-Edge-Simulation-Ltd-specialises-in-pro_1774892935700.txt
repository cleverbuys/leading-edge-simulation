About Us

Leading Edge Simulation Ltd specialises in providing high quality simulation services and solutions to a range of UK and international customers engaged in diverse flight training operations.
Based in Northamptonshire in the East Midlands, we offer a range of services from onsite FSTD maintenance and performance testing, authority evaluation support, remote support by phone or email, to hardware repair at our premises in the UK.
We are JOSCAR registered.

Our Capabilities

With our in depth understanding of flight simulation technical challenges and with unique insight into realising customer aspirations in the areas of FSTD upgrades, maintenance and support, we can offer flexible, efficient and affordable solutions to operators.  We can also provide advice and consultancy on all aspects of flight simulation and training solutions.

Our core areas of product expertise include:
Repair & maintenance of Motionbase Plc motion platforms
Support & maintenance of all cueSim Ltd flight simulator devices
Repair & maintenance of 3D Perception projectors
Configuration & calibration of visual display systems
FSTD QTG performance tests and authority evaluation consultancy and support


Out Team

With access to a number of multi skilled domain experts, each with other 30 years experience in delivering simulation products and services, we can provide agile, dynamic and responsive services to meet customers’ deadlines and operational requirements.
